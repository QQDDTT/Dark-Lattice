## The Regression of Knowledge Premiums

For thousands of years in human civilization, the differentiation of classes depended heavily on the monopoly of professional knowledge. Complex knowledge systems like law, medicine, and high-level mathematics formed the moats of the elite class.

However, AIOS has made this \"depth of knowledge\" cheap. Through LUI and Agents, an ordinary person who has not received professional legal education can defend their rights by calling a supreme court-level legal Agent via natural language.

## Social Impacts of Intellectual Equality

1. **Devaluation of Professional Skills**: So-called \"professionals\" who survive solely on their mastery of information (such as junior coders, translators, and basic accountants) will face a catastrophic decline in remuneration. Professional knowledge has shifted from an \"asset\" to a \"public utility."
2. **Dismantling of Cognitive Barriers**: The threshold for innovation has fallen back from \"possessing a skill\" to \"possessing an idea.\" This provides unprecedented opportunities for upward mobility for those at the bottom, provided they possess deep insight.
3. **Reshaping of Class Mobility**: Future social status will no longer depend on \"how much you know,\" but rather on \"how much Agent compute power you can mobilize\" and the \"depth of your intent modeling."

## The \"Tap Water-ization\" of Intelligence

Knowledge is transforming from an expensive luxury locked in ivory towers into a basic public resource like water and electricity. Although this process of equalization is painful, it heralds the arrival of a new human civilization centered on **\"Creative Intent.\"**
