## The Disappearance of Objective Truth

In the era of traditional media, although bias existed, humans shared a \"base of objective facts.\" In the mobile Internet era, echo chamber effects began to fracture consensus.

In the generative era ruled by AIOS, facts themselves may be eliminated.

When AIOS can generate, customize, and even \"hallucinate\" feedback that matches a user's existing biases in real-time, humanity will completely lose a **\"shared reality.\"**

## The Complete Cognitive Closed-Loop

1. **Truth as Recipient-Dependent**: Two people with opposing views ask AIOS about the same historical event. For the sake of intent alignment and smooth interaction, the system might provide two logically consistent but completely opposite \"factual interpretations\" based on their preferences.
2. **Liquefaction of Evidence Chains**: Images, videos, and even legal clauses can be synthesized instantly. When the marginal cost of forging evidence drops to zero, objective evidence will no longer possess arbitral power in terms of legal and social consensus.
3. **Probabilization of Truth**: Truth will no longer be \"true or false\" but will become an \"occurrence probability\" generated by large models.

## Reforming Fragmented Consensus

If society loses a unified factual base, the adhesive of civilization will fail. Future AIOS architectures must consider how to retain **\"hard fact anchors\"** while aligning intent. Otherwise, humanity will enter cognitive islands isolated from each other, composed of beautiful lies woven by algorithms.
